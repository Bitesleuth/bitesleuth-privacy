Privacy Policy for BiteSleuth

Effective Date: May 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BiteSleuth collects the following information:

  • Account information: Email address (for authentication via Supabase)
  • Meal data: Text descriptions, photos, and barcode scans you upload for nutrition analysis
  • Usage data: Meal history, recipe saves, and premium subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Analyze meals using OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini API
  • Process payments through Stripe
  • Store your data securely via Supabase
  • Provide personalized nutrition insights

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered food analysis (text and photo)
  • Stripe: For processing premium subscription payments
  • Supabase: For user authentication and cloud data storage
  • Netlify: For hosting the app and serverless functions

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing zennixfoodlabs@aol.com. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

5. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: [ZennixFoodLabs@aol.com]
